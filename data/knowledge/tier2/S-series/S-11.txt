CATEGORY : Structural
SUBJECT : Balconies, Decks, Projections, and other Exterior Elevated Elements
PURPOSE : The purpose of this Information Sheet is to summarize the requirements in the
current code for balconies and decks.
REFERENCE : California Building Code (CBC), Current Edition
San Francisco Building Code (SFBC), Current Edition
San Francisco Housing Code (SFHC)
DISCUSSION :
The purpose of this information sheet is to identify code provisions applicable to the design and
construction of balconies, decks, projections, and other exterior elevated elements.
California Building Code
Sections 107.2.5 and 110.3.7 include provisions requiring information and details on construction
documents for an impervious moisture barrier system of exterior elevated elements and associated
inspection requirements during construction.
Table 1607.1 provides minimum live loads of balconies and decks for structural design.
Section 2304.12.2.4 includes detailing requirements and specifications for wood structural members that
support moisture-permeable floors and roofs that are exposed to weather.
Section 2304.12.2.5 includes provisions for ventilation at the underside of enclosed framing in exterior
elevated elements.
San Francisco Building Code, Section 1402.8 Projections and Appendages
Section 1402.8 stipulates the requirement to control rainwater backflow under projections, as well as the
requirement for ventilation at all enclosed spaces of projections in wood framed construction. This section
also provides provisions for drainage at uncovered balconies and decks.
San Francisco Housing Code, Section 604 Structural Maintenance – Affidavit Required
San Francisco Housing Code, Section 604 requires submission of the Compliance Affidavit to the
Department of Building Inspection’s Housing Inspection Services for periodic structural inspection and/or
