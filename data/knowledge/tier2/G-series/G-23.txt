SUBJECT : General

TITLE : Products acceptance

SS SR I EY
REFERENCE : San Francisco Building Code (SFBC)

PURPOSE : To establish criteria for acceptance of products for construction

DISCUSSION : The following products for construction are acceptable:

1. Products that have current evaluation report based on data submitted in accordance with an
approved acceptance criteria (AC) or evaluation criteria (EC), where such criteria are published
pertinent to the product. Evaluation reports shall be from one of the following organizations:

a. International Code Council (ICC) Evaluation Service; Evaluation reports link: https://icc-
es.org/evaluation-report-program/
b. International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials ((APMO) Uniform Evaluation

Service; Evaluation reports link: http:/Awww.iapmoes.org/EvaluationReports/default.aspx

Other products will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis by the Supervisor or Manager.
