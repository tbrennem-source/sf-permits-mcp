Data Accuracy

This is not legal advice. Permit timelines, fee estimates, and routing predictions are based on historical data and statistical models. Always verify critical information directly with the San Francisco Department of Building Inspection (DBI) before making business decisions.

We aggregate public permit data from data.sfgov.org and update our database nightly. However:

  • There may be a lag between DBI updates and our database refresh.
  • Timeline estimates are statistical predictions based on historical permit patterns — individual permits vary significantly.
  • Fee estimates are calculated from published DBI fee schedules and may not reflect special circumstances or recent updates.
  • Entity data is derived from permit contact records and may contain errors or duplicate records.
  • All permit data is public record sourced directly from the City and County of San Francisco.
