Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

What We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide the service:

  • Permit search queries — logged for service improvement and debugging. Retained 90 days.
  • Email addresses — collected at signup for account access, magic-link authentication, and optional morning brief emails.
  • Usage analytics — page views and feature usage via PostHog (see Third Parties below). IP addresses are anonymized.
  • Uploaded PDFs — plan sets uploaded via the plan analysis tool are processed in memory and not stored permanently. No PDF content is retained after analysis completes.
  • Watchlist addresses — properties you choose to watch are stored in your account so we can alert you to changes.
  • Feedback submissions — bug reports and suggestions you submit via the feedback widget.

How We Use It

  • Service delivery — returning search results, generating property reports, sending morning briefs.
  • Search improvement — anonymized query logs help us understand what users search for and improve result quality.
  • Analytics — aggregate usage data helps us understand which features are most useful.
  • Beta access management — email addresses are used to manage beta invitations and account tiers.
  • Email communications — we send magic-link login emails, morning brief emails (opt-in), and significant policy update notices.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not share personally identifiable information (PII) with third parties for their own marketing purposes.
  • We do not store uploaded plan PDF files permanently — they are processed in memory and discarded.
  • We do not track your activity across other websites.

Data Sources

All permit, property, business, complaint, violation, and inspection data is sourced from public San Francisco government records via data.sfgov.org. This data is public record and updated nightly. We do not create or modify government records — we aggregate and analyze them to make them more accessible.

Third Parties

We use the following third-party services to operate sfpermits.ai:

Service Purpose Data shared
PostHog Product analytics Anonymized page views and feature usage events
Railway Cloud hosting and database All application data (processed in their infrastructure)
Anthropic AI processing via Claude API Permit data and plan content sent for analysis (not your personal info)
SendGrid Transactional email delivery Your email address for login links and brief emails

Data Retention

  • Search query logs — retained 90 days for service improvement, then deleted.
  • Account data (email, watchlist, preferences) — retained until you request deletion.
  • Uploaded plan PDFs — not stored; processed in memory and discarded immediately after analysis.
  • Feedback submissions — retained indefinitely to track product improvements.

MCP Server (Claude.ai Integration)

When you connect sfpermits.ai to Claude.ai via our MCP server, tool calls are processed by our server and responses returned to your Claude conversation. We log tool call metadata (endpoint name, timestamp, response time) for rate limiting and debugging purposes, but we do not log your Claude conversation content.

OAuth tokens issued for Claude.ai integration are tied to your sfpermits.ai account. You can revoke access at any time by contacting us.

Cookies

  • Session cookie — required for authentication. Expires when you close your browser or log out.
  • PostHog analytics cookie — optional. Used to track aggregate usage patterns. You can disable this in your browser settings.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing privacy@sfpermits.ai with the subject line "Delete my account." We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

You can unsubscribe from morning brief emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in the footer of any brief email, or by updating your account preferences.

Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: privacy@sfpermits.ai
